What is a Personal Mainframe?

A personal mainframe is your home server that seamlessly integrates with all your devices, acting as a central hub for your digital life. It's like having your own private cloud, but with complete control and privacy.

Seamless Integration

All your devices become terminals to your mainframe, sharing the same environment and data.

Smart Processing

Heavy computations and sensitive operations are handled by your mainframe, keeping your devices light and secure.

Autonomous Backup

Your data is automatically backed up to your mainframe, ensuring you never lose important information.
